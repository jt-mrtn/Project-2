About JT

I began my career as a professional musician performing all over Europe and the USA. I enjoy playing classical and Irish traditional styles on flutes and whistles. I also like gardening and riding bikes, hiking, skiing (XC & downhill), and running. Eventually, my mathematically-curious brain led me to exploring how computers do all of this magic. So naturally, I became a programmer.

As a software developer, I strive to reach timely and elegant solutions for my client's needs. A programmer affects a person's work life so I take that role very seriously. I provide applications to enhance your productivity.

I was a report writer and software applications developer at the Smithsonian for 12 years. I gained lots of enthusiasm for programming, especially for the web, so I sought out Thinkful to hone those skills even further. So far, so good! So let's get going on some exciting projects!
