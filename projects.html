Kaleidoscope Eyes

Throughout time we've asked ouselves: what came first, the chicken or the egg? There are so many facets to that question. So much so, it's like a cultural diamond in the rough.

I've taken this age-old quandry and explored its imagery, but more specifically, how the golden ratio is the driver, its mysterious, ever-present, Fibanacci-sequence engine that incessantly asserts itself into our lives.

Another shape similar in form is the human eye. Like chicken eggs, the width is just about 1.6 times the height, on average. I don't think The Beatles had this in mind when they wrote Lucy in the Sky with Diamonds but they did talk about eyes...

Clicked on the link and still in the dark? You poor thing. If you caress and by caress I mean press your mouse buttons individually, you'll encounter different moods. Use all of them, even your center one (scroll wheel). Sway your little plasticine gnawer to and fro. What happens? Go fast or slow. Hold the button of your choice down. What happens then?

For the right mouse button, press the Esc key to get rid of the pop-up menu. And by all means, feel free to reload the page to start your psychedelic trip all over. Have fun!
