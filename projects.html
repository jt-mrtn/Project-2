Kaleidoscope Eyes

Throughout time we've asked ouselves: what came first, the chicken or the egg? There are so many facets to that question. So much so, it's like a cultural diamond in the rough.

I've taken this age-old quandry and explored it's imagery, but more specifically, how the Golden Ratio is the driver, its mysterious, ever-present, Fibanacci-sequence engine that incessantly asserts itself into our lives.

Another shape similar in form is the human eye. Like chicken eggs, the width is just about 1.6 times the height, on average. I don't think The Beatles had this in mind when they wrote Lucy in the Sky with Diamonds but they did talk about eyes...

Clicked on the link and still in the dark? You poor thing. If you caress and by caress I mean press your mouse buttons individually, your eyes might reward you differently. Use all of them, even your center one (I'm talking about the mouse buttons). That center one should be your scroll wheel if your electronic rodent is so endowed. Sway your plasticine little gnawer to and fro. What happens? Go fast or super slow. Hold the button of your choice down. What happens then?
